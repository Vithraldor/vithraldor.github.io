My time with Toronto Defiant

Building a great community

Community hero banner. Art by frogsmile.

A little background: Overwatch is a team-based game where two teams of six compete against each other to complete certain objectives. Toronto has a professional Overwatch team representing the city: the Toronto Defiant. Behind this professional esports team lies a great and vibrant community, filled with people that come from a variety of backgrounds.

I've been a part of this community for a little over 2 years, having participated in various events like meet and greets, community game nights, and watchparties. However, in summer of 2020, I was presented with the opportunity to give back to the community and make an impact on Toronto's Overwatch scene in my own way.

I was given the opportunity to moderate the Toronto Defiant community, serving as an authority figure that intervened whenever problems arose within the community, or serving as a role model for other members. I was also given the chance to host events with a group of other passionate individuals: ranging from community game nights to community tournaments.

This hard work has not gone unnoticed: Toronto Defiant recognized me as a community hero for running these events and banding the community together. A blurb about my contributions can be found here.
